KinConform

Machine learning / AI software to predict the activation status in protein kinases, a superfamily of signaling proteins essential for life. Trained using 3,365 high resolution kinase crystal structures curated from the Protein Data Bank (PDB) and manually annotated by kinase experts, this classifier achieves over 99% accuracy on 2,365 validation structures. Created using Python's SciKit-Learn and MDAnalysis packages.
